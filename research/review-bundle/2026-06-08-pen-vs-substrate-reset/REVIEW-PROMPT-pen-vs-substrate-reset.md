## Grounding (field check — lean on the known, spend on the departure)
Before reinventing: a quick literature pass (per the brief's research-before-hand-rolling).
- **The "swappable pen" is a named, emerging design pattern** — "identity in memory rather than model
  weights, the LLM as a swappable reasoning engine" (LLM-agent literature). But the paper that formalizes
  agent-identity metrics — *Agent Identity Evals* (arXiv 2507.17257: identifiability / continuity /

  to our regime, not a quirk.
- **Parallax has a 65-year backbone: MTMM / "multiple operationism"** (Campbell & Fiske 1959) — triangulate
  across operations each "approximate, fallible, and independently imperfect," and read **convergent
  validity** (self-axes agree) *and* **discriminant validity**. That gives the surface/register control a
  formal job: it is the **discriminant** check — it must NOT track the self-axes. The irreducibility-warning
  is the pre-registered split. So parallax here = MTMM with a named home for non-convergence.
