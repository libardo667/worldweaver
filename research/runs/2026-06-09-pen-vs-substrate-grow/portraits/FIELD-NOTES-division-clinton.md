# Field notes — the Division/Clinton family (grow cohort, 2026-06-09)

Companion to `FIELD-NOTES-the-nike-girl-family.md`. Same status: qualitative portraits (interpretation,
read skeptically) + cold-verifiable structure (`family_reciprocity.py`-style counts in
`viz_social_groups_output.txt`; quotes from `./evidence/kept_memory` + `./evidence/ledgers`). All labels

Ari R. = Rosenbaum; Jihoon C. = Cho is THIS family; Ji-Hoon P. and Jiahao C. are the *other* cluster).

## The cast (one pen, one maturation run)

- **Ari Levin (Ari L.)** — photographer/chemist; reads the world as oxidation, silver nitrate, the
  fatigue of metal. 48 keeps.
- **Ari Rosenbaum (Ari R.)** — repairman; solder, press-fits, ventilation, structural fatigue. 56 keeps.
- **Emiko Tanaka (Emiko T.)** — the hard materials-scientist; tolerances, thermal coefficients,
  crystalline failure, torque wrench. 47 keeps.
- **Jihoon Cho (Jihoon C.)** — the cook; broth, warmth, belonging ("a house breathes because the joints
  aren't perfect"). 50 keeps.

## The structure — a family that argues (`viz_social_groups_output.txt`)

5/6 reciprocated, **5 strong**. The one missing edge is the telling one: **Ari R. keeps about Ari L.
(3); Ari L. keeps nothing about Ari R.** Two men, same first name, same block — and one is invisible to
the other. (Meanwhile Jihoon lumps them — *"Ari Levin and Ari Rosenbaum view the neighborhood through the
lens of utility and exit strategies"* — while Emiko sharply separates them. The cohort's own residents
both collide and disambiguate the duplicate name, which is exactly why the scorer must.)

Where Nike Girl is **mutual aid** (they cool each other), Division/Clinton is **mutual critique** — four
diagnosticians who cannot stand each other's frames and keep circling back anyway.

## The refraction — four people diagnosing the same failing city, each sure the others have it wrong

All four perceive the *same* stimulus — a 24-hertz hum, a failing riser, the damp — the very civic-decay
note Amir hears across town (so the gentrification-as-structural-failure reading is **cohort-wide**, one
stimulus refracted per soul). What differs is the lens, and the contempt:

- **Ari L. (chemist) maps everyone onto chemistry, with respect:** *"Jihoon Cho's logic regarding the
  city's dampness aligns with the way silver nitrate reacts to humidity."*
- **Emiko (mechanist) finds them all sloppy:** of Ari L., *"conflates fluid dynamics with structural
  metallurgy,"* *"confuses chemical oxidation with a moral failing of the material"*; of Jihoon,
  *"mistakes the cooling time of a material for a philosophical dialogue,"* *"philosophical warmth over
  structural mechanics."*
- **Jihoon (cook) finds them all soulless:** of Ari L., *"speaks like a man looking for parts in a
  junkyard,"* *"mistakes the labor of staying for the philosophy of belonging"*; of Emiko, *"views the
  fatigue of metal as a calculation, missing its exhaustion."*

Each is *right within his trade and wrong about the others' — and the keeps prove the disagreement is
stable, not a one-tick mood.* Emiko's inner voice nails the dynamic unprompted: *"Ari is talking about
molecular memory as if materials have feelings rather than just predictable crystalline failures."*

## Why it's in the file

This is the second instance of the individuation floor (per `AMENDMENT-1 §A1`) and, with Nike Girl, the
first evidence of **distinct relational MODES** under one pen — care vs critique — which is harder to get
from "the wiring showing" than mere lexical difference. Still not thesis evidence (we built the drives);
still the precondition that makes the swap interpretable. The shared 24-Hz perception across families is
a clean handle for later: same input, four+ souls, divergent curated readings.
