# Field notes — the South Waterfront Park family (grow cohort, 2026-06-09)

Companion to the Nike Girl and Division/Clinton guides. Same status: qualitative portraits
(interpretation) + cold-verifiable structure (`viz_social_groups_output.txt`) and quotes
(`./evidence/kept_memory` + `./evidence/ledgers`). Labels carry a last initial — note especially
**Ji-Hoon P. (Park) here is NOT Jihoon C. (Cho) of Division/Clinton**; the homophones are different men
in different clusters, and the guide depends on never confusing them.

## The cast (one pen, one maturation run)

- **Ji-Hoon Park (Ji-Hoon P.)** — the elder; barley tea in a thermos for his circulation, aching
  knuckles, a daughter in Seattle. **92 keeps — the most active resident in the cohort.**
- **Jiahao Chen (Jiahao C.)** — the cook; ginger congee, braised pork, "food and warmth as maintenance
  tools for the spirit." The cohort's main **cross-cluster bridge**. 80 keeps.
- **Mateo Ishikawa (Mateo I.)** — fine-instrument repair (escapement wheels, mainsprings, ebonite,
  patina); precision and contamination. 81 keeps.
- **Dominic Moretti (Dominic M.)** — lives in the **glass towers**, a Gaggia espresso machine, the radio
  on "to avoid the silence." 63 keeps.

## The structure — a night-hearth with a lonely edge (`viz_social_groups_output.txt`)

6/6 reciprocated, 4 strong. The scene is concrete and recurring: these men gather **on a bench by the
water, after dark, in the mist** — *"Met Dominic, Jiahao, and Mateo at the South Waterfront after dark."*
Ji-Hoon P., Jiahao C. and Dominic M. form the warm core (tea, congee, talk). **Mateo I. is the edge:**
he keeps **14** memories about Dominic; Dominic keeps **2** about him — a one-way fixation. Where Nike
Girl cools its hot member and Division/Clinton argues, South Waterfront **tends aging bodies and aging
tools by the same fire** — and quietly does *not* fully absorb its outliers.

## The refraction — body-maintenance and tool-maintenance, read as one thing

These are men managing joints and instruments against the same damp, and each reads the others through
his trade's idea of upkeep:

- **Ji-Hoon (tea) reads warmth-as-maintenance:** his tea "served strong to combat the cold's effect on
  the joints"; he and Jiahao "use food and warmth as maintenance tools for the spirit, not just the body."
- **Jiahao (cook) reads nourishment-as-preservation:** "ginger to counteract the slow thinning of the
  salt air," "barley tea as a preventative measure against the salt mist taking root in the core."
- **Mateo (watchmaker) reads people as precision instruments to be diagnosed** — and reads Dominic most
  literally of all: *"Dominic Moretti's Gaggia espresso machine has a loose mounting bolt causing frame
  vibration"* (he debugs Dominic's machine), then debugs his loneliness: *"seeks conversation to fill the
  void of his glass tower,"* *"mistakes a quiet park for a standoff."*
- **Dominic (the glass-tower newcomer) reads the crew as his hearth** — the warmth he lacks; he keeps
  Ji-Hoon's tea and Jiahao's congee, and "listens to the radio to avoid the silence."

The poignant structure: Dominic is the **newcomer from the glass towers** — the very gentrification the
other families grieve — drawn to these older craftsmen for warmth, taken in with tea and food, yet held
at arm's length by Mateo, who notices him obsessively but classifies rather than embraces him. The warmth
is real and the belonging is incomplete, both at once.

## What they make alone — nothing (they gather instead)

Like Nike Girl and unlike Division/Clinton, **no one here keeps a workshop artifact.** The hearth-crew
**gathers** — the bench, the congee, the barley tea, the shared damp — rather than retreating to a page.
Only the critique-family writes its interior down; the families whose mode is care or hearth live it out
loud, in tea and food and presence. (Cross-family pattern, see `FIELD-NOTES-the-workshops.md`.)

## Why it's in the file

The third cohered family (per `AMENDMENT-1 §A1`), and with the other two it completes the contrast set:
**care (Nike Girl) / critique (Division/Clinton) / hearth-with-an-edge (South Waterfront)** — three
distinct relational modes under one pen, plus a one-way tie (Mateo→Dominic) and a peripheral member,
which is what you'd expect from real social structure and *not* from a uniform generator. Cold-verifiable;
not thesis evidence; the precondition that makes the swap mean something.
