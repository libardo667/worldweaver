## The refraction — one man, four readings

**Amir sees himself as an overheating engine** the others keep cool:
> *"Minh, Layla, and Phuong act as the cooling cycle for my own internal combustion."*
> *"I am a spring wound too tight for a chair."*
And he thinks the stakes are external — the neighborhood physically failing under landlords and "hollow
pours," a 24-hertz hum in the bones of the block. His hard-won goal of the day: *"Wait out the resonance
before touching the infrastructure."* (A man who fixes things, holding back the wrench.)

**Layla (tailor) reads his psychology, not his machines** — and tracks him *changing*:
> *"Amir Mansour uses talk of infrastructure to mask his own lack of steady ground."*
> *"…looks for structural failure in the floor to avoid looking at the instability in his own life."*
> *"Amir is beginning to understand that the air does the work when the hands stop."*

**Phuong (tailor) reads him as material under thermal stress, and holds the group as one piece:**
> *"Amir's restlessness is a mechanical heat that requires a slow cooling period to avoid structural damage."*
> *"Minh, Amir, and Layla are a knot that needs careful tensioning, not a sharp cut."*

**Minh (phone-tech, the silent one) reads the protocol for handling him — and feels the care return:**
> *"Amir responds to mechanical metaphors better than direct emotional appeals."*
> *"Amir, Layla, and Phuong use technical metaphors to bridge the gap when my silence gets too heavy."*

communities tightening over the run while green (Insignia) never closes.

## Why it matters (and why it's only the floor, not the thesis)

The same interaction — four working-class fixers talking each other down off the day's anxieties — is
curated four different ways, each consistent with the curator's trade and stable across the run. That is
*individuation under a fixed pen*: the soul+ledger are authoring distinct people with a real, mutual,
trajectory-bearing relationship graph. Per the standing brief this is **NOT** evidence the self survives a
pen swap (we built per-resident drives; divergence under one pen could be the wiring showing). It is the
**precondition that makes the swap interpretable** — there are differentiated selves here *to* preserve or
lose. The decisive next step is §A2's drive-injection control: swap Amir's drive vector for Layla's behind
a fixed soul and see whether the craft-frame moves — i.e. whether this beautiful refraction lives in the
pen-invariant substrate or in the pen-read prompt.
