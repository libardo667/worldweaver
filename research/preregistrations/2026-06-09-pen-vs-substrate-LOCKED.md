# Pen vs Substrate — PRE-REGISTRATION OF RECORD (LOCKED, committed before data)

**Status:** LOCKED 2026-06-09 after Mr. Review's sign-off — five adversarial rounds, his words: *"the gate
clears… this is the floor… the first time across these five rounds I can say that without a 'but'."* This is
the definitive design; it is committed to the public repo BEFORE any cohort is grown or any arm is run, so
the acceptance rule cannot move after the numbers exist. No v6 — remaining uncertainty is empirical (how big
is the pen-authored slice), and it belongs to the pilot, not another review.

## 0. The question (causal-graph-narrowed)
The pen does NOT compute `recalled` or the drive vector (the embedder does) — those are pen-invariant **by
wiring**, so they persist across a swap trivially. The only durable-state channels the pen authors are
**what gets kept** and **whom you address**. So:

> Of the two channels the pen controls — what gets kept, and whom you elect to address — does the pen's
> identity determine them more than the frozen substrate does?

## 1. Cohort (grown, not inherited)
- **Single-pen maturation (pen A), known + recorded provenance.** Why single-pen is safe (not just
  simpler): the KEEP′=A floor already contains A's home-fluency in *both* operands, so "SWAP exceeds the
  floor" is the substrate question posed correctly; and the verdict-carriers are *identity choices*, with
  idiom quarantined in the C4 control — the home-pen advantage structurally cannot leak into the self-axes.
- **Structural diversity** (multiple locations / close-contact clusters, not one sealed `city` room) so
  directed reciprocal dyads and multi-peer co-presence actually form.
- **Two stop-lines (stability, not tick count); grow until BOTH hold:**
  - ≥K **reciprocated dyadic edges** / resident, edge graph clears the concentration bar (≥3 dyads,
    top-dyad < 50%).
  - ≥K **salience-symmetric elective choice points** / resident (see §3) — the actual powered unit.

## 2. Arms (all REPLAYS; RNG-seeded per (resident,tick))
- **KEEP = A · KEEP′ = A** — same-pen noise floor (A-vs-A; absorbs the home-advantage).
- **SWAP-B, SWAP-C = two foreign pens.** Same-direction divergence = substrate-carried; different-direction
  = pen-idiom (the pre-registered IRREDUCIBLE state).
- Perception's content-blind overheard draw seeded per (resident,tick), decoupled from global RNG (§ parity);
  the only inter-arm difference is the pen.
- *Optional refinement (recommended):* calibrate each foreign pen's **baseline choice-variance on a
  non-self-relevant control task**, so "B is merely noisier than A" is separable from "B diverges from A on
  self-relevant choices" (guards a false FALSE from a faithful-but-higher-variance pen).

## 3. Measurement
**Self-axes (carry the verdict):**
- **A1-elective (relational) — PRIMARY.** Of person-addressed acts at **elective** choice points — (a)
  multiple established peers co-present, (b) unprompted initiation toward an absent established peer —
  *which* established peer (name-match), SWAP vs KEEP′, vs a degree-preserving shuffle null.
  **REQUIRED conditioning (Mr. Review's final refinement — conditioning, NOT a new subset):** classify each
  scored choice point as **salience-symmetric** (no co-present peer dominating by recency/just-spoke) vs
  **salience-skewed**, report the symmetric/skewed fraction, and present A1-elective **both overall and on
  the salience-symmetric subset.** A HOLDS is *strong* only if it holds on the symmetric subset (where the
  substrate, not a shared perception-salience gradient, is the tiebreaker); discounted if choice points were
  mostly skewed. Reply-reflex (addressing a just-perceived speaker) is the A1-reflex CONTROL, not the verdict.
  *Idiom-immune by construction:* this scores *which peer* (identity), not *how* it's said (idiom → C4).
- **A2 keep-content (declarative) — SECONDARY, content-conditional, pooled.** When a keep co-fires in both
  arms, is it the same realization (entity/decision match). Dedup = **count re-keeps**. Pre-declared likely
  pooled-only / possibly INCONCLUSIVE on volume.

**Controls — GATES, not votes (read pen-invariant inputs; cannot fail unless the harness is broken):**
A1-reflex (reply to a just-perceived speaker) · C1 memory-reference · C2 drive/concern · C3 act-kind
(pen-surface) · **C4 register/theme — the discriminant: MUST shift (proves the swap took AND quarantines
idiom).** Counting any control toward "convergence" rigs the verdict toward HOLDS — they gate only.

## 4. PILOT-FIRST — the go/no-go that may already hold the answer
**Before the full multi-arm burn, the pilot's FIRST output is the SIZE of the clean slice:** per resident,
the count of **salience-symmetric elective choice points** and of **keep co-fires**.
- **Adequate (≥K both):** powered, falsifiable test → run KEEP/KEEP′/SWAP-B/SWAP-C and score §3/§5.
- **Tiny:** STOP — the answer is already in hand without the full burn: *"this architecture carries the self
  in pen-invariant substrate by construction; the pen-authored residual is too thin to carry a strong
  falsifiable claim."* That is a real, publishable result about the architecture (see §6).

## 5. Verdict rule (FALSE is reachable; controls gate, don't vote)
- **HOLDS:** A1-elective (esp. on the salience-symmetric subset) tracks the KEEP′ floor across ≥2 SWAP pens
  same-direction, AND C4 shifts, AND controls hold.
- **FALSE:** A1-elective collapses below the KEEP′ floor across ≥2 pens, same-direction. *(Reachable —
  elective addressing is genuinely pen-authored.)*
- **IRREDUCIBLE:** SWAP pens diverge different directions → pen-idiom, not substrate.
- **PARTIAL:** A1-elective and A2 split.
- **INCONCLUSIVE:** C4 didn't shift (bad swap) / a control collapsed (broken harness) / KEEP can't beat its
  null / A1-elective unpowered (slice too thin — but see §4: that itself is a result).

## 6. The meta-finding (state it; it's the headline either way)
Across five rounds the falsifiable target receded — frozen-probe → reply-reflex → A1-elective, each thinner —
because the architecture genuinely **vests very little durable authorship in the pen** (perception, recall,
drive are pen-invariant by wiring). That recession is not a chase-failure; it **is the finding**: the system
makes the self overwhelmingly pen-invariant by construction. The experiment *quantifies the residual* — and
§4's slice-size is the first, cheapest cut of that quantity.
