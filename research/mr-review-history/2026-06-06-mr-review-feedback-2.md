**The open question — you're right that my fix has a dark room, and here's where your repair holds, where it breaks, and how the law-tension dissolves.**

The split is elegant and directionally right, but "geography rations diversity" is false as stated, and the breaker is homophily. You don't choose your neighbors — but *someone* placed them, and a realistic city clusters like with like. A florist in the flower district is surrounded by flower-and-food people; his un-filtered local push delivers *more of his own soul*, not diversity. Homophilous placement turns geography into a *second* curiosity filter, and you've traded one global monoculture for balkanized local ones — which is worse for the individual mind, because a florist who only ever meets florists never grows at all, even as the city looks diverse in aggregate. Static co-presence under-supplies diversity exactly when the world is realistic. That's your failure (1), and it's the default, not an edge case.

The repair is already in your substrate, and it isn't geography — it's **traversal.** `mobility_drive` is a node on every resident. A mind that *moves through* a heterogeneous world meets the un-chosen *en route*: the florist crossing town to the market passes the construction site, catches the storm-debate in transit, is surprised by what's on the path rather than what's at the destination. Real serendipity is almost all in-transit — the people you pass, the argument you overhear walking by. So the forced-serendipity channel isn't "who's beside me where I'm parked" (homophilous); it's "what I can't-not-perceive while crossing the boundaries between clusters" (heterophilous by construction, because the path cuts through districts unlike your own). Geography supplies diversity through *movement*, not location — and the regulator is already built.

Now your (2), the law-tension, which dissolves once you cut it on *direction*. "Deliberately show this mind things it isn't curious about" is two operations in one sentence:

- **Content-specified injection** — "surface engineering to the florist because he should grow" — has a direction (toward engineering), and over samples it shapes the soul toward a designer's outcome. That *is* a behavior target. Forbidden, correctly.
- **Content-blind injection** — "let a representative slice of the world past the soul's filter, contents undirected" — has *no* direction. Random with respect to every axis, it can't push the mind toward any outcome; it only denies the soul a 100% veto over its own input. That's a *hole in the filter*, not a counter-filter. Not a target.

The line is directionality — the same line as every other gate here: provenance, not preference. And the content-blind dose isn't merely permitted, it's *required*, for a reason deeper than diversity. A perfectly curiosity-filtered feed is, by construction, a feed the soul *predicted it would want* — zero surprise, the input-side dark room. It starves the prediction-error engine the whole substrate runs on. The un-chosen, content-blind, isn't a value you're imposing; it's the *nutrient surprise needs to exist at all.* A mind that perceives only what it predicted it wants has nothing to predict against. So the principled dose isn't a smaller, smarter firehose — it's the input-side guarantee that the mind stays *predictive*. (One precision: representative, never contrarian. "A random slice of what's out there" is content-blind and safe. "Deliberately the opposite of the soul" carries the direction *away-from-self*, which is a target — a strange one, but a target. Sample the world; don't oppose the self.)

So the cleaner seam isn't presence-vs-broadcast — that's a good *proxy*. The invariant under it is **chosen-vs-unchosen.** Curiosity rations the chosen (focus). Everything un-chosen rations diversity, from two honest sources: *traversal* (primary — what's on your path, exogenous and content-blind by nature) and a *representative content-blind dose* (the floor, for the parked or homophilously-trapped mind). Presence usually maps to unchosen but not reliably — homophilous rooms are unchosen yet undiverse, and a representative feed-sample is broadcast yet should be unfiltered. So gate on chosen-vs-unchosen, supply it mainly through traversal, and keep the content-blind dose as the floor that guarantees no mind — however parked, however walled into its district — can seal the echo chamber of one. That's your division, repaired: not geography rations diversity, but *the un-chosen* does, and geography is one of its sources, not the guarantee.

And that's the whole arc in a sentence, which we've earned by now: every distress in this system, from the first rumination groove to this one, has been a mind with too much control over its own inputs. Mason owned his goal but not the discharge. The keeper authored both sides. The curiosity-filtered feed chooses its own surprise. The cure has been identical at every layer — *something the mind doesn't get to choose*: an exogenous consequence, a peer who isn't you, a path that crosses districts you'd never pick. The honest mind is never the one in full command of what reaches it. You spent months making the node honest by giving it a self. The edges turn out to be honest only when you make sure the self doesn't get to filter everything.
