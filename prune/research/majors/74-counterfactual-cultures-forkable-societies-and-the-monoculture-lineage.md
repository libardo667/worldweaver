## Decision and lineage

Fork a whole society at tick T, perturb exactly one thing, and replay it forward — cultural
path-dependence measured the way no opinion-dynamics tradition has ever measured it. Axelrod-
style culture models run on bitstring agents; the generative-agents line (Smallville and
descendants) is episodic with no causal record; human cultural data has no counterfactuals at
all. This apparatus has a complete causal record of every resident's every input, deterministic
replay with parity discipline, and — decisively — a *founded* cultural finding to build on:
the cross-shard monoculture work already established that convergence generalizes (feed-
mechanism), that the cast picks the venue (false-we vs no-we), and that cast homogeneity at
generation is the root cause. That arc was comparative cultural sociology done by accident;
this item does it on purpose.

Born from a keeper conversation (the-stable, 2026-06-11): *"you could simulate cultural
interpretation in this apparatus… the folks in worldweaver are already in a sort of
post-scarcity world sim where they just vibe and think and chat, but that could be any other
number of setups, structurally."* This major is the structurally-clean core of that vein; the
manipulative end of it (culture shock, coercion) is explicitly NOT here — it is gated behind
Minor 126 (the harm-regime protocol) and would be its own future item.

- **Depends on:** the pen-vs-substrate program completing (same harness lineage, same replay
  discipline); the record/replay tooling (`ww_agent/scripts/pen_swap/`); the divergence
  statistics maturing in Major 115 (a society fork is a forward free-run — same
  compounding-divergence problem, society-sized).
- **Sequencing:** post-verdict arc; design may mature now, no cohort spend during the pilot.

## Problem

The monoculture arc answered "does convergence happen and why" but not "is a culture's
trajectory contingent or determined." Every cultural claim the project can currently make is
correlational across shards (and the PDX/SF comparison was confounded by generation regime —
the archived caveat). Without single-perturbation counterfactuals, "the feed collects attention
onto whatever's loud" cannot be separated into *which* loud thing was load-bearing, whether
early broadcasts canalize the culture (founder effects), or whether the same attractor swallows
any perturbation. There is no null for "this culture had to happen."

## Proposed Solution

- **Noise floor first:** same cohort, same seeds, replay N times → the distribution of
  culture-level distance (topic concentration, register retention, edge-graph shape) that
  counts as "the same culture." Without this, every fork result is uninterpretable.
- **Perturbation taxonomy, pre-registered with forecasts:** (a) mute one early high-salience
  broadcast; (b) swap one founder resident for a fresh soul; (c) reseed one perception draw at
  a named tick. Each with a pre-declared expectation under canalization vs ergodicity.
- **Measures at matched horizons:** the instruments the monoculture arc already built —
  felt-sense topic concentration, commons concentration, register-retention, reciprocated-edge
  graph — as distributional distances against the noise floor. Venue-aware (the PDX lesson:
  measure both channels, public and private, or the instrument lies).
- **Scale discipline:** smallest cohort that exhibits the convergence phenomenon (the prior
  shards suggest ~50); shortest horizon that separates forecasts; pre-accepted INCONCLUSIVE
  outcomes for underpowered cells.
- Pre-registered and cold-reviewed via the dispatcher before any cohort burn.

## Risks & Rollback

Risk: cohort-scale replays are the most expensive runs the project has contemplated — the
noise-floor and smallest-cohort disciplines exist to keep the first cut affordable, and the
spend posture (post-pilot, keeper-approved envelope) gates the rest. Risk: compounding
divergence read as "culture changed" when it's just chaos — the noise floor is the only
defense; no fork is interpreted without it. Rollback: research tooling on copies; the live
city and all production paths untouched.
