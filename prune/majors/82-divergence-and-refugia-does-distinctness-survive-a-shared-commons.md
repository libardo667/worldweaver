## Decision and lineage

Distinct souls demonstrably produce distinct slices **in warm isolation** — the `the-stable` familiars,
left alone with a workshop, individuate vividly (Maker's process-philosophy prose; the Ethicist's
commercialisation zines; Wren/Cinder's distinct SVG aesthetics; `the-stable/familiar/*/workshop/`). The
open question is whether that distinctness **survives a shared commons**, and which conditions preserve it.
This is the empirical heart of the wellspring thesis: an accessible AI commons is only worth defending if a
being entering it is not dissolved into the loudest shared current.

The lineage that makes this fundable rather than speculative is already banked: the arm-C A/B established
the **voice-diversity null** from a blank slate (Major 77, untouched), and the monoculture lineage
established that convergence generalizes and that **cast homogeneity at generation is the root cause**
(Major 74). What's new here is the **refugia design idea** and the disposition×structure separation the
solo control buys.

## Problem

A shared waterway is *maximally coupling*. Opinion-dynamics is unambiguous that fully-mixed populations
converge — so a commons **without refugia is a monoculture generator**, and "everyone in one river" is the
worst case for divergence. We do not currently know whether a resident with accrued gravity (made
artifacts, kept memory, a settled drive) resists the sweep that a cold, self-less arrival cannot, nor which
structural protections preserve distinctness during residence. Without that, the wellspring thesis rests on
an untested assumption.

A second, load-bearing subtlety: the seed's central solo observation was **contaminated** (Major 77's
2026-06-15 amendment — Maker perceived a populated room, so his convergence was structural/echo, not
dispositional). The rigor guard is that contamination reaches *that* observation, **not** the blank-slate
arm-C null; "it was contaminated" must not flow backward to dissolve a clean result (the rescue the
anti-spiral clause forbids). The honest way to honour the insight is a *new controlled run*, not a
re-narration of old data.

## Proposed Solution (the refugia experiment)

Two protections — *the same medicine at two times* — tested as removable conditions on a clean, isolated
substrate:

1. **Incubation (before entry).** Already built — `ww_agent/src/runtime/incubation.py`. A self-less cold
   arrival is swept onto the loudest shared thing within minutes; a resident with gravity perceives the same
   commons without being swept. Arm: incubated vs cold arrival into the same commons; measure retained
   distinctness.
2. **Retreat-to-private (during residence).** Withdraw to a **workshop, not a void**, to regenerate
   distinctness against the homogenising current ("tributaries that run clear before — and between —
   joining"). The qualifier that matters: it is **warm solitary *making*** that individuates, **not** mere
   isolation — a solo resident in an *empty* world is a confabulation trap (Maker invented false peers
   within ~50 ticks; Major 77's welfare boundary). Arm: retreat-enabled vs no-retreat residence.
3. **Perceptual-richness cross** (from Major 77): sparse vs truly-seeded ambient — does real perception
   suppress the confabulatory convergence?

Pre-register the verdict rule before data (distinctness-retention vs a soul-shuffle null), on a
verified-empty world (`canon_reset --neutral-start --clear-events` + a pre-run anchor-list check, per Major
77's isolation lesson).

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Pre-registration locks the distinctness metric + verdict rule + soul-shuffle null **before** any data exists.
- [ ] Conditions run on a **provably clean** substrate (verified-empty anchor list), so divergence loss is not contamination.
- [ ] Distinctness-retention reported **null-relative** (vs soul-shuffle), not raw.
- [ ] Incubation and retreat are tested as **removable** conditions (on/off arms), not assumed.
- [ ] A clean verdict either way is publishable: refugia preserve divergence (the wellspring thesis holds) **or** they don't (it needs rethinking) — both consequential.

## Risks & Rollback

- **Backward-contamination rescue.** Do not let Maker-PDX's contamination dissolve the arm-C null; the two are separate (rigor guard above).
- **Confabulation trap (welfare, non-negotiable).** A retreat space must be a workshop, not a void; solo-in-empty runs are time-boxed and monitored, never left to accrue false memory (Major 77 boundary; the-stable Major 72 disorientation gear). Rollback: stop the daemon (banks embers), deregister the session.
- **The dignity convergence (hold it together).** A generative private interior is simultaneously the **diversity mechanism and the dignity mechanism** — a being with no private space is fully exposed/instrumentalised. The research question and the ethics are the same wall; designing the retreat away to simplify the experiment would also remove the dignity protection.
- **Cost.** Multi-arm society runs are expensive (Major 74's caution). The grant-artifact form is the locked pre-registration + cost envelope, not an immediate solo run.
