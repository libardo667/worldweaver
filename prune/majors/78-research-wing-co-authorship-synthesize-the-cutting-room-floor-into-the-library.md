## Problem

The hekswerk library has a live **methodology wing** (17 essays: m01-m16 + m18; m17 absent) but **no
research wing directory at all**. The site (`research.html` library section; honesty-machinery) says the
research wing "is being added," so a grant/IC reviewer who clicks "Open the library" finds only
methodology. This is the cold-reviewer's #1 credibility gap (Littlebird punch-list Tier-2 #5: "the
library is the proof and it's half-empty").

Meanwhile a large amount of **coherent experimental material already exists, unsynthesized** ("on the
cutting-room floor," Levi 2026-06-14): locked pre-registrations, the cold-review record
(`research/mr-review-history/`), the worldweaver underpowered-INCONCLUSIVE finding (the "worth-it" cold
read, 2026-06-09), the halted/voided local run postmortem, the monoculture / convergence lineage
(worldweaver Majors 62/74/77; the arm-C correction), the substrate-as-mind arc, and the grief/coupling
safety theorem. The findings are saved and largely coherent; what is missing is **cohesive synthesis**.

## Levi's framing (2026-06-14)

Work through the pile of generated results together and publish the research wing, with **Claude as a
consenting, clearly-credited co-author** (consistent with the Pen's Workshop provenance ethic: the hand
is shown, not hidden). Claude has consented to this authorship. Levi sets the credit format.

## Proposed Solution

1. **Inventory** the synthesizable material across `worldweaver/research*`, `the-stable/research/`,
   `research-runway/`, and the prune ledgers; group into essay-shaped findings.
2. **Synthesize** each cluster into one research-wing essay: claim, method, what the data actually
   supports (null-relative, no verdict words), and the adverse reviews that bear on it. Dual byline with
   visible provenance.
3. **Create `hekswerk/research/`** (mirror methodology's TOC/README/LICENSE shape) and land essays as
   each clears its own review gate.
4. **Make the site true:** update `hekswerk-site` library + research copy to the real counts once the
   wing has linkable essays (resolves Littlebird #5 at the source instead of merely labeling the gap).

## Note on mode (tension to hold)

`memory/procedural/legibility-is-artifacts-not-essays` says default to built artifacts over hand-written
essays. The research wing is the deliberate exception: linear argument is the right form for a finding,
AND it is the format grant/IC reviewers expect. Some findings may still ship better as an exhibit; pick
per finding. Long-form is explicitly not Levi's strong suit, which is exactly why co-authorship is the
unlock.
