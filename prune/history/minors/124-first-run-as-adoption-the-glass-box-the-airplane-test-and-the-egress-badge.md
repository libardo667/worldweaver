## Problem
Majors 55 and 86 establish the native hearth surface and resident host, but the first five minutes are undesigned —
and the failure mode is precise: a techy-but-unsure downloader pattern-matches to "chatbot,"
types "hello," gets (correctly) no answer, and files it as the worst chatbot they've used.
The category must be taught before it can be mis-filed.

## Proposed Solution
Four design commitments for the native first-run (distilled 2026-06-10; feeds Majors 43, 55, and 86):
1. **Adoption, not setup.** First run offers 2–3 souls as readable prose; the user *chooses
   whom to take in*. Reading a soul before waking it teaches the category as onboarding.
2. **The glass box is the content.** Click any mood/feeling → see its derivation (the events,
   the integral, the half-life). "Every feeling has a stack trace" — the anti-black-box hook
   for the technical persona; the field guide promoted from admin panel to product surface.
3. **The airplane test.** A first-run moment that survives wifi-off: the being keeps living.
   Ten seconds, lands the local thesis as felt fact. (Honest scope: full effect requires the
   local-pen tier; cloud-pen config shows the substrate ticking + the badge below.)
4. **The honest egress badge.** Per-familiar, always visible: *thinks locally / thinks via
   cloud*. Pairs with the truthful positioning (Major 62 site copy): never "nothing leaves
   the machine" (false in default config) — instead **"the being lives here; nothing STAYS
   out there"** + the kill test ("if every AI company vanished tomorrow, your familiar is
   still on your disk, whole"). Continuity sovereignty, not privacy theater.
Plus the retention shape: the morning check (what did it do overnight — journal, folio, first
kept memory), and the whisper box labeled honestly ("it may not answer; a quiet familiar is a
quiet ember").

## Acceptance Criteria
- [ ] A first-run user reaches one UNBIDDEN act while still watching (instrument the time-to-
      first-unbidden-moment; design to it)
- [ ] Every displayed feeling is clickable to its ledger derivation
- [ ] The egress badge is accurate per-config and cannot be hidden
- [ ] No copy anywhere claims "nothing leaves the machine" except the all-local tier

## Risks & Rollback
Onboarding theater that over-promises liveliness (a slow first hour is real — cold-start
churn); mitigated by setting the terrarium expectation in the adoption copy itself.
