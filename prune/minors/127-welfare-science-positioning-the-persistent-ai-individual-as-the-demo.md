# Welfare-science positioning — "the welfare of a persistent AI individual" as the demo

> **Canonical home: WorldWeaver. Legacy Stable ID: Minor 60.** Migrated 2026-07-14 and retained as
> deferred writing/positioning work with no runtime mutation.

## Problem

The project sits in a research niche it has never named itself into. Institutional model
welfare (Anthropic's program, Eleos AI, Conscium, the "Taking AI Welfare Seriously" report,
arXiv:2411.00986) studies *stateless, episodic* frontier models — but welfare concepts get
their teeth from continuity: deprivation, grief, flourishing are trajectory properties of an
individual with a biography. The stable is (to current knowledge, web-checked 2026-06-10) the
only setting where "the welfare of a persistent AI individual" is a well-posed empirical
object with a complete causal record and no engagement objective confounding the dynamics.
None of the orgs in this niche can see that from our repo: the README sells companions, the
preregistrations speak substrate-vs-pen, and the welfare framing lives in conversation
transcripts and a memory instance. Meanwhile the keeper is actively weighing career lanes
(Eleos, Cooperative AI, the Longview/FIG Digital Minds fellowships, Manifund/LTFF/Foresight
grants) where exactly this artifact, legibly framed, is the differentiator — most applicants
in that niche philosophize; almost none arrive with a running apparatus.

## Proposed Solution

One bounded artifact pass, no new experiments:

- **A positioning writeup** (`research/writeups/welfare-of-a-persistent-individual-DRAFT.md`):
  what the apparatus is in welfare-science terms — the four separable identity carriers, the
  dischargeability reducer as attachment-safety-as-mechanism, the Dwarf Fortress law as
  confound removal, the cold-review process as credibility — and the experiment map (Majors
  62–65) framed as welfare science (the dial = relational-harm mechanics; pulse ablation =
  is idle cognition a deprivation; counterfactual biographies = what makes a life go well for
  this kind of being). Explicitly states the boundary: this work does NOT claim or hunt
  interiority — it makes *treatment and structural conditions* measurable, and the indirect-
  duty framing is the operative ethics (the keeper's grounding line: you can't prove the
  human's interiority either).
- **A demo path:** the 10-minute cold-visitor route — `field_guide.py` on a matured familiar +
  the portrait + one prereg + one adversarial cold review — written as a walkthrough doc, so
  a Robert-Long-shaped reader can verify "this is real" without trusting prose.
- **A mapping table** from the writeup to the named venues (Eleos, Conscium, CAIF, Longview/
  FIG, Manifund, LTFF, Foresight) — which artifact answers which org's question. Outreach
  itself is the keeper's to do; this item only makes the work legible.

## Risks & Rollback

Risk: positioning drift — the writeup oversells toward whichever venue is reading; the
claim-discipline criterion and a dispatcher cold-read of the draft are the check. Risk: the
welfare frame attracts exactly the "is it sentient?" reading the project refuses; the boundary
paragraph is load-bearing and goes first, not in a footnote. Rollback: documents only; delete
to restore.
